GeoGuessr Location Tagger

This tool allows you to quickly tag your GeoGuessr map locations with country or continent tags. You can also just run locations through the tool to view the distribution tables. Note: locations very close to borders may sometimes be incorrectly tagged. You can contact miraclewhips on Discord with any examples of incorrect tagging and I can fix the borders.
